About the Event

“SRiJaN 2.0” is the National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Summit organised by E Cell NIT Silchar. It is a unique initiative to motivate and educate people about entrepreneurship and to provide a meeting ground for Corporate experts and young budding entrepreneurs from NIT Silchar as well as several distinguished institutions across the country.

Organized by the Institute Innovation Council & Start-up Centre, NIT SILCHAR in association with IEEE ComSoc NITS Student Branch Chapter

About NIT SILCHAR

National Institute Of Technology, Silchar (राष्ट्रीय प्रौद्योगिकी संस्थान, सिलचर) is one of the 30 National Institutes of Technology of India and was established in 1967 as a Regional Engineering College in Assam. In 2002 it was upgraded to the status of National Institute of Technology and was declared as Institute of National Importance under the National Institutes of Technology Act, 2007.

Outwit the Board Room

We understand it’s difficult to come up with an idea, but an idea is not all you need. Put your analytic skills to the test and improve on a pre-existing idea. On being allotted a well-established business, tune in with your team and discuss strategies to outsmart their ideas. Find ways to avoid making the same mistakes, and develop strategies to one-up their ideas.

Objective/Outcomes

The event forces the team to research and dive deep into the business model of a given company, tests their analytical observations and promotes critical thinking and teamwork. It also promotes creativity and working well within guidelines, which are important skills as an entrepreneur. Most of all, it promotes a spirit of improvement and learning based on others' experiences. It promotes a better understanding of finances, and promotes decision making under the pressure of knowing those decisions that affect people beyond their comprehension.

Panel Discussion

Having a brilliant mind is great but having the ability of learning from the problems of experienced ones is the best habit one can nurture. Here we are with "Panel Discussion'' where real CEO, COO, CFO of various startups and companies will discuss the problem faced by them in running and raising a firm and they will also share some secret mantras of tackling such tough situations.Times is definitely money but experience is the eternal treasure.

Objective/Outcomes

Panel Discussions are far more fruitful compared to a speaker session. Seeing great minds interact and share their thought process gives an insight into their expertise. It is very lucrative for a budding entrepreneur to have their doubts discussed and cleared by a panel of experts.

Pitch Please

After a great idea, comes the execution. To know the real value of your idea, it is important to pitch it to someone with experience. Come forth and prove your merit to a panel of judges, and make sure you are pitch-perfect.

Objective/Outcomes

Pitching an idea requires an in-depth understanding of every aspect involved. It forces the participants to question their idea and analyse every step they take. A well-planned pitch is the most essential step to gain investors, and is an essential skill for all entrepreneurs.

IPL Auction

The Trill,the suspense, the bids, and the AUCTION!! Build your dream team and your passion to form the best out of your favourite players. Bring out your craze for cricket and set your bidding strategies with your mates.

Objective/Outcomes

The objective of IPL Auction is to let the participants place bids and win them. The participants will be exposed to a real environment where they will be making decisions and strategize things in the heat of the moment to grab maximum points against other bidders

Bid & Get Started

Ever heard of any auction where you can select your startup team? Here we are, an auction with a bit of spice!! Get yourself ready to collaborate with your team for a fresh startup.

Objective/Outcomes

This event opens up an opportunity for startups to find suitable collaborators, and for specific individuals to find a team of their choosing. It opens up opportunities for collaboration, and helps each participant develop a better understanding of their niche.

Business Quiz

One of the best ways to test your knowledge about a topic is to Quiz yourself on it. To help motivate you, we bring the Business Quiz. An event based solely on garnering knowledge about all things business, and delivering your best on all questions asked.

Objective/Outcomes

Business Quiz provides a platform for all enthusiasts to test their knowledge and compare it to their peers. A student needs to have a curious mind, always eager to learn, and being able to access their performance in comparison to others with similar interests promotes a healthy competitive spirit. It is also a great platform for students to engage with those having similar interests and expand their network.

RESUME your business

What are the two most important things to have a successful career? Skills and a good CV, right? What if we take care of both these things for you, and all you have to do is show up to the interview. Give your best with a CV you’ve seen for 5 mins, and make sure to think well on your feet!

Objective/Outcomes

The event promotes creativity and on-the-spot thinking. Coming up with quick witted replies, that humour the audience is a key ability needed to impress investors. Analysis and making the best out of a situation promote critical thinking.

TechNerds

An idea is the most important step of the journey, but it is also often the first step. After an idea comes the implementation, the skills required to convert your idea into a product that can be offered to the audience is often more important. So, bring in your team and use your skills to solve some problems commonly faced by entrepreneurs.

Objective/Outcomes

Technical skills are an integral part of any company, all companies require a team of talented people who will develop their ideas into viable products and fix any bugs along the way. The analysis of the problem statement, and the time-rated implementation of a solution needs a lot of focus and talent.

Stock Market Investing

The stock market has garnered the interest of all age groups recently. This event gives a chance to the participants to analyse the trends in the stock market and trade. The aim is to study the trends and maximize your profits.

Objective/Outcomes

This competition promotes the analysis of trends, knowledge of stocks and intelligent investments. Stock Market Investments are a great way to understand data analysis and trends, and make predictions based on that understanding.

Intern Fair

Every student wishes to have an opportunity to explore the corporate world, and every company is looking for talented individuals to contribute to their product. We plan on bridging the gap between the two groups by providing an opportunity for both groups to explore the other.

Objective/Outcomes

It provides a platform for companies to hire India’s best talent for different profiles as well as a platform for students to get an internship or a placement in these companies. It allows students to understand the needs of the current industry and provides them with a platform to show off their skills.

Speaker Sessions

It is important to have an inspiration, someone to look upto. It is also important to have someone who has been on the same journey and can aid you with their experience. Our speakers will do just that, entrance you with their experiences and eliminate all your fears. This exclusive opportunity to experience their journey through their words and live their experiences is going to leave everyone entranced.

Objective/Outcomes

Experiences are the best lessons. It is important for the students to not make the same mistakes others have, in order to progress at a much faster rate. It is essential for all students to envision their goals and be aware of a path to reach them. This leads to an improved experience, and enhances the student’s ability to learn from the experiences of others.

Digital Marketing

If you’re wondering what digital marketing is, it’s not just building network assets like Facebook Pages, Instagram Accounts, YouTube brand channels, blogs, websites etc., just because everyone seems to be doing so. Unplanned strategy or improper execution consequences into a collection of disconnected things that dispense or disorient the brand and confuse the clientele. As an entrepreneur or an aspiring digital marketer, it is essential that before you execute anything, you strategize, integrate and well- coordinate all offline and online assets.

This workshop will help you keep your brand’s identity intact as customers move across different platforms and help you ensure that each platform contributes incrementally to the brand’s value.

You will have a clear understanding of the tools, techniques and the knowledge required to develop proper marketing strategies and execute effective marketing campaigns.

Cyber Security Workshop

Cyber security is the act of safeguarding protecting data, networks, programs and other information from malicious attacks.

The cybersecurity industry is rapidly growing every day, with new technological developments comes new threats. Although more resources are being deployed to counter cyber-attacks, the nature of the industry still has a long way to go before we can, as a whole, catch up with these threats.

Here are some Alarming Cyber Security Facts and Stats:

  • There is a hacker attack every 39 seconds.
  • On average, only 5% of companies’ folders are properly protected.
  • Since COVID-19, the US FBI reported a 300% increase in reported cybercrimes
  • Approximately $6 trillion is expected to be spent globally on cybersecurity by 2021


Do you feel vulnerable that your data may go in wrong hands?

Here at SRIJAN 2.0 the cyber security workshop we will assist you on how to save yourself from various types of cyber threats & will also act as a stepping stone to an exciting career in Cyber Security.

Entrepreneur Essentials

There are certain things that an aspiring entrepreneur needs to be aware of. Turning an idea into a viable venture, validating ideas through structured experiments, building a team, refining their business strategy, and raising the capital necessary to create value and grow their business. There is a specific skill set involved in being an entrepreneur, and we aim to provide a glimpse into it to all students.

Objective/Outcomes

Entrepreneurs need to be aware of the challenges they face, and have the skills necessary to overcome them. Being confident and taking charge, owning your mistakes and making improvements are just a few of the necessary skills. Our workshop aims to acquaint the entrepreneurs with these requirements and help them fulfill it.
